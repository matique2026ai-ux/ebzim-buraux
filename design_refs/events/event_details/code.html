About the Gathering

A cinematic celebration of our roots, the Grand Cultural Heritage Gala is an immersive exploration of the Maghreb’s architectural and artisanal legacy. Hosted within the storied halls of the Sétif Opera House, this evening bridges the gap between the echoes of our ancestors and the innovations of the next generation.

Attendees will experience live traditional orchestration, a curated exhibition of archival textiles from the Ebzim Collection, and a sensory journey through historical gastronomy.
