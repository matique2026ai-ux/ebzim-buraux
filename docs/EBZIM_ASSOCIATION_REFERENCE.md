# EBZIM Association Reference

## Source Authority
- This document is derived from the **Amended Bylaws (القانون الأساسي المعدل)** of the EBZIM association, approved by the General Assembly on **14 December 2024**.
- This file is the **authoritative operational reference** for all user-facing copy, institutional language, and product terminology.
- All future agents **MUST** consult this file before creating or modifying any text related to the association's mission, identity, or governance.

## Official Identity
- **Official Arabic Name**: جمعية ابزيم للثقافة و المواطنة
- **Official Latin/Brand Name**: EBZIM
- **Nature**: Provincial Cultural Association (جمعية ولائية ذات طابع ثقافي)
- **Territorial Scope**: Sétif Province (ولاية سطيف), Algeria.
- **Orientation**: Non-profit, public interest, focused on national constants and values.

## Core Mission Summary (Based on Articles 03 & 04)
The association aims to تسخير معارف ووسائل الأعضاء بصفة تطوعية (harness members' knowledge and means voluntarily) to:
- **Protect Heritage**: Safeguard the tangible and intangible cultural heritage of Sétif and Algeria from theft and disappearance.
- **Foster Dialogue**: Promote cultural and artistic exchange within the province and with other institutions (national and international).
- **Cultivate Citizenship**: Integrate the concept of citizenship through cultural action and expand social responsibility toward heritage.
- **Support Youth**: Protect youth and children from deviation and drugs through art, dialogue, and ethical cultivation.
- **Preserve Memory**: Use digital technology and media to preserve popular memory, the history of the Algerian Revolution, and national identity.

## Main Thematic Pillars
1. **Culture & Arts**: Promoting all forms of art in schools and society; discovering and mentoring emerging talents.
2. **Citizenship & Society**: Raising civil awareness, promoting civilized dialogue, and respecting state institutions.
3. **Heritage & History**: Protecting historical, architectural, and urban landmarks; restoring monuments and martyrs' cemeteries.
4. **Memory & Revolution**: Glorifying the image of the Algerian Revolution and its heroes; preserving national memory.
5. **Technology & Innovation**: Integrating modern digital tools in heritage monitoring and protection.
6. **Tourism & Economy**: Developing non-profit cultural tourism by activating the economic role of cultural production.

## Product Writing Rules
- **Institutional yet Modern**: Use language that reflects a formal association but feels accessible and "digital-first."
- **Professional & Clear**: Avoid heavy bureaucracy; focus on impact and participation.
- **Arabic First**: Arabic is the source of meaning. Always ensure the Arabic copy is primary and culturally accurate.
- **Heritage-Grounded**: Use terms that resonate with "Identity," "Memory," and "Authenticity" (الأصالة، الذاكرة، النوعية).
- **Non-Political**: Maintain a strictly cultural and civic tone as per the bylaws.

## Membership vs Account Rule
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Account != Membership**
> - Creating an account in the EBZIM App provides access to digital services and activities.
> - **Official Membership (انخراط رسمي)** is a separate legal process defined by Article 11 (written request and approval by the executive office).
> - UI copy must clearly distinguish between a "User Profile" (ملف مستخدم) and an "Official Member" (عضو رسمي).

## Approved Terminology (Arabic)
- **Association Name**: جمعية إبزيم للثقافة والمواطنة
- **Official Member**: عضو رسمي / عضو ناشط
- **Membership Application**: طلب انخراط
- **Cultural Heritage**: الموروث الثقافي / التراث الثقافي
- **National Memory**: الذاكرة الوطنية
- **Citizenship Action**: الفعل الثقافي والمواطنة
- **Emerging Talents**: المواهب الصاعدة
- **Martyrs' Heritage**: ذاكرة الشهداء والمقاومة الشعبية

## Avoid / Prohibited Terminology
- **DO NOT USE**: "Political Party" or any political labels (الجمعية غير سياسية).
- **DO NOT USE**: "Commercial/Profit" terms (الجمعية غير مربحة).
- **AVOID**: Overly generic terms like "Club" (use "Association" or "Committees").
- **AVOID**: Inventing titles like "CEO" (use "President" or "Executive Office" terms).
