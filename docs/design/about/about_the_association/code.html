Our Story

A Legacy Rooted in the Soil of Algeria

Founded in the historic heart of Sétif, Association Ebzim (إبزيم) emerged from a collective desire to safeguard the intangible treasures of our region. The name "Ebzim" refers to the traditional ornamental fibula—a symbol of connection, adornment, and identity across North African history.

What began as a small circle of researchers and craftsmen has evolved into a leading civic institution dedicated to the documentation and revitalization of Algerian cultural heritage. We believe that preservation is not a static act, but a dynamic dialogue between generations.
